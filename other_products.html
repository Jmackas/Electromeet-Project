Proctoring

The proctoring module attached to the Electromeet software allows for instructor invigilation of all students simultaneously. It is capable of performing the following:

  • Assessing students at home or work
  • Assessing performance in remote lab
  • Desktop snapshots and video/audio record
  • Video/audio surveillance with integrity
