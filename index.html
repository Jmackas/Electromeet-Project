What is Electromeet?

Electromeet is software you can use to join live online sessions. Use it for meetings, eLearning, remote laboratories, product training, remote support, and more. You can join or provide live sessions in real time.

First 30 Days Free

In an Electromeet session you or the presenter(s) can:

  • Talk to the presenter(s) and other participants.
  • Use instant text for questions and comments.
  • Draw and annotate on the whiteboard and use a slide show.
  • Watch video.
  • Take a conducted 'web tour'.
  • Participate in remote laboratory sessions.
  • Record a session for later viewing.

It is like being in a "virtual room" with other people. It is a room that's available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Electromeet aims to be simple, effective, flexible, and reliable. It is the online platform that you'll soon master, freeing you to focus on the content of your online work.
