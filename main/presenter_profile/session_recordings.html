Session Recordings

Presenters in Electromeet sessions are able to make recordings of sessions. These recordings are stored on the website for 45 days from the end of the session.

Below, you will see a list of recordings (Saved Sessions) made with your Provider Licence. The last part of the link name includes the date and time of the recordings. Times are recorded in GMT/UTC (UK time), and the dates are in US format; MM/DD/YY.


Current United Kingdom Time


There are two methods available for retrieving your recordings:

  1. Download the recording file(s) directly; click file name in the last column in the display table to download the recording and Save it to your hard disk. Note that some recording files are large and if you are planning to email them out to participants they may be too big to send as attachments.
  2. Receive an email with a unique link to your chosen recording(s) Select the recording(s) and then click Send Mail. A link for the recording will be sent to you that you can forward it by email to selected participants. Links contain the same times that you can see here within the link body text. The participant can click on the link to download the file directly. Once participants have a copy of the recording file, they can play back the recording by launching Electromeet and going to File >Open Saved Session >[browse to saved session location].
